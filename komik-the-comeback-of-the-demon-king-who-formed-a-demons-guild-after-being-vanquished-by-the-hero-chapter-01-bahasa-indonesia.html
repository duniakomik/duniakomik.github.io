1. Who was the name of the king?Apakah jawabannyaThe name of the king was .....??? Tolong jawab iya atau tidak


Jawabannya adalah "Ya".

PEMBAHASAN

Ketika kita menjumpai penggunaan to be dalam sebuah pertanyaan, maka kita harus menggunakan to be yang sama sesuai dengan pertanyaannya. Tapi hal ini juga tetap harus diperhatikan siapa subject nya. Contoh :

1). Is Mr. Aldi our English teacher? (Apakah Mr. Aldi adalah guru bahasa inggris kita?)

Yes, Mr. Aldi is our English teacher. (Ya, Mr. Aldi adalah guru bahasa Inggris kita)

2). Where are they? (Dimana mereka?)

They are in the living room. (Mereka ada di ruang keluarga)

3). Who are You? (Siapa kamu?)

I am a new student here. (Saya adalah seorang siswa baru disini) Nah, pada contoh ini karena pertanyaannya menggunakan "kamu". Maka tidak mungkin, kita jawab menggunakan subject "kamu" lagi.

Jadi, untuk kalimat pertanyaan di atas

Who was the name of king? (Siapakah nama raja?)

The name of King was.... Pertanyaan ini menjelaskan tentang nama raja sebelumnya (sebelum di rubah namanya), oleh karena itu to be yang digunakan adalah bentuk past "was".

Soal:

Choose the correct option!

Jawaban:

1. B.

2. A.

3. B.

4. B.

5. A.

Penjelasan:

1. Future Tense

(+) S + WILL + VERB 1 + O

Active: I will clean the house every Saturday afternoon

Passive:

(+) S + WILL BE + VERB 3 + By ...

b. The house WILL BE CLEANED by me every Saturday afternoon ✅

2. Present Continues

(+) S + is/am/are + VERB+ing + O

Active: The workers are finishing the work now.

Passive:

(+) S + is/am/are + BEING + VERB 3

a. The work IS BEING FINISHED now ✅

3. Past Tense

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

Active: Dewa performed the songs amazingly

Passive:

(+) S + was/were + VERB 3 + By

b. The songs WERE PERFORMED by Dewa amazingly ✅

4. Past Continues-Passive

(+) S + was/were + VERB 3 + By

b. Our bookselves WAS BEING REPAIRED by the carpenter when you came ✅

5. Present Perfect-Passive

(+) S + has/have + been + VERB 3 + By

a. The house HAS BEEN by that family ✅

32. determine the size of the angle formed by the hour 02.40​


Jawab:

160 degrees.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

To determine the size of the angle formed by the hour hand and minute hand of a clock at 02.40, we can use the following formula:

angle = |30H - 11/2M|

Where:

H is the hour (in 24-hour format)

M is the minute

At 02.40, H = 2 and M = 40. Substituting these values into the formula, we get:

angle = |30(2) - 11/2(40)|

= |60 - 220|

= |-160|

= 160

Therefore, the size of the angle formed by the hour hand and minute hand of the clock at 02.40 is 160 degrees.

Jawab:

Pada pukul 02:40, jarum jam telah berpindah dua pertiga dari 2 ke 3, yang setara dengan (2/3) * 30 = 20 derajat.
