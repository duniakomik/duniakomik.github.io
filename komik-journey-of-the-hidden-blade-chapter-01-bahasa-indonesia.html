Simple Present Tense:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

[tex] \frac{45}{100} \: = \: \frac{18}{x} [/tex]
[tex]x \: = \: 18 \: \times \: \frac{100}{45} [/tex]
[tex]x \: = \: 2 \: \times \: 20[/tex]
[tex]x \: = \: 40 \: km[/tex]
